Sun Studio

Rihanna. Kanye West. The Script. James Vincent McMorrow. WILL.I.AM. Tony Visconti. Stephen Street.

Sun is a studio environment for those looking to get top-of-the-line sounding records. Its tranquil and professional vibe allow you to perform at your best, and have a great time doing it.

There’s also a pool table to have fun and wind down!

Studio One

The Clockworks. The Hot Sprockets. Whitetrash. Æ MAK. Rónán Connolly.

Studio 1 is Dublin’s hidden studio gem. Many successful artists from the Irish music scene have started out their careers by recording here.

With its state-of-the-art equipment and unique sounding Wood-Floor Live Room, Studio 1 is perfect for musicians who are looking to get a foothold in the music industry.
